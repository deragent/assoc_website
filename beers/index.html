About the Octanis Racks

What this event is about

Learn about how a science project takes flight outside of academia or industry. Jonathan Sobel, a researcher from Hackuarium, an open laboratory in Renens, talks about his project to map beers at the molecular level, as well as about how he launched and manages this project. Learn about DYI biology in general and DNA extraction in particular, launching your own independent research project, and about the intermediate results of the beer metagenome project!

When and where?

Oct 3rd, 19h30 in MED 3 2215.
