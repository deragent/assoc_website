Tech Specs

Don't forget to check out the wiki if you'd like to know more about the technical details of the rover.

Sensors

  • Sensirion SHT21 (Temperature)
  • Bosch BME280 (Temperature)
  • Bosch BMP180 (Temperature)
  • Bosch BNO055 (IMU)
  • UVB Sensor (IMU)
  • AMS 6xxx8 (Lightning/Storms)

Modules

  • RockBLOCK Iridium Messenger
  • u-blox 6m GPS receiver
  • OceanOptics Spectrometer

Power, Motors

  • SunPower C50 cells
  • Maxon Motors
  • CHINA Motors

Materials

  • PTFE
  • PU
  • PS
  • Steel
