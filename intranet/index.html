Members Refund

Octanis members can claim a refund when paying for association related expenses. These expenses have to be formerly validated by the Board. If you want to claim a refund for Octanis related expenses, fill the Refund Form. If you forgot to upload the expenses files proving the expenses while filling the form, you can upload them via the Upload Payment Proof button directly.
